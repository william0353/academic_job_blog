About Us

Liaad is a website that monitoring vacancies from all the universities and research institutions. The data is being updated in minute level.

All the data are directly gotten from the origin source, no third parties.

This website is fully accessible for free registered users. In addition, user could choose a premium membership. By paying annual fee, user could define several keywords. When a job post contains the keywords appeared in the internet, users could get email notification within few minutes.
